About Me

Bridging Industry Practice with Academic Excellence

I am an accomplished cybersecurity professional and academic researcher specializing in applied cryptography, post-quantum security, and network engineering. Currently serving as Senior IT Operations Technician and SAP Technical Consultant at EPI-USE Labs, I bring extensive experience from Pearson PLC, The Egyptian Banks Company, and IBM.

As a prolific researcher, I have authored 15+ publications in high-impact Springer and MDPI journals, co-edited a Springer book on Cybersecurity & AI, and achieved 63 citations with an h-index of 6.

Notable achievements include winning the ISACA London Cybersecurity Competition representing Northumbria University and serving as an active council member of ISACA and IET. My expertise spans cloud architecture, advanced cryptography, blockchain technology, AI/ML implementation, and enterprise SAP solutions.
